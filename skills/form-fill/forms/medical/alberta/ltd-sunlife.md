# Sun Life — Disability Claim Medical Report (LTD)

Sun Life's Disability Claim Medical Report is completed by the attending physician for long-term disability claims under Sun Life group insurance. Sun Life is one of Canada's largest group benefit providers.

## Insurer-Specific Notes

- **Form title**: "Disability Claim Medical Report" — Sun Life's standard name
- **Narrative-heavy**: Sun Life's form allows more narrative than Manulife's — physicians can describe limitations in their own words rather than checkbox grids
- **Specialist referral tracking**: Sun Life specifically asks about pending and completed specialist referrals with dates
- **Pre-existing condition focus**: Sun Life's form includes explicit questions about whether the condition existed before coverage began

## Section Structure

1. **Patient and Claim Information** — name, DOB, employer, certificate number, occupation
2. **Diagnosis** — primary diagnosis with ICD-10, secondary diagnoses, date of onset, date first consulted
3. **Pre-existing Condition** — was the patient treated for this condition before the coverage effective date?
4. **Treatment History** — current medications, therapies, surgical history, specialist referrals (dates and outcomes)
5. **Functional Limitations** — narrative description of what the patient cannot do, with examples from daily activities and work tasks
6. **Prognosis and Recovery** — expected duration, is the condition improving/stable/deteriorating, return-to-work estimate

## Common Pitfalls (Sun Life–Specific)

- Skipping the pre-existing condition section — Sun Life uses this for eligibility determination; an omission delays processing
- Providing only a diagnosis without functional limitation narrative — Sun Life's adjudicators rely on the narrative more than most insurers
- Not listing specialist referral dates — Sun Life tracks the referral-to-assessment timeline as a treatment adequacy signal

## Terminology

- "Plan Member" or "Employee" = the insured
- "Certificate Number" = Sun Life's equivalent of policy/member number
- "Case Manager" = Sun Life's assigned disability management contact
