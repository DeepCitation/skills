# Manulife — Attending Physician Statement (STD)

Manulife uses the same Attending Physician Statement (APS) form for both STD and LTD. During the STD benefit period, the APS should emphasize acute presentation, recovery trajectory, and return-to-work planning.

## Insurer-Specific Notes

- **Same form as LTD**: Manulife's APS is a single form used across benefit periods
- **STD focus**: Adjudicators expect recovery-oriented answers — prognosis in weeks, active treatment plan, specific return-to-work dates
- **Elimination period**: Typically 0–7 days under Manulife group STD plans

## Section Structure

Same as `ltd-manulife.md`. STD-specific framing:

- **Prognosis**: Give recovery timeline in weeks. Include graduated return-to-work dates if applicable.
- **Functional abilities**: Describe current limitations AND expected improvement trajectory
- **Treatment**: Emphasize time-limited interventions with review dates

## Common Pitfalls (STD-Specific)

- Framing the condition as permanent or long-term on an STD claim
- Missing return-to-work date — Manulife STD adjudicators require this field
- Not noting whether the employee can perform modified duties

Same as `ltd-manulife.md`.
