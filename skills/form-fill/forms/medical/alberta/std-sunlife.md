# Sun Life — Disability Claim Medical Report (STD)

Sun Life uses the same Disability Claim Medical Report for STD and LTD benefit periods. During the STD period, the report should emphasize acute presentation and recovery trajectory.

## Insurer-Specific Notes

- **Same form as LTD**: Sun Life's medical report is used across benefit periods
- **STD focus**: Recovery timeline in weeks, specific return-to-work date, graduated return options
- **Elimination period**: Typically 0–14 days under Sun Life group STD plans

## Section Structure

Same as `ltd-sunlife.md`. STD-specific framing:

- **Prognosis**: Recovery timeline in weeks. Include graduated return-to-work dates.
- **Functional limitations**: Describe current state AND trajectory — "currently cannot lift >5 lbs; expect return to 20 lbs within 6 weeks"
- **Pre-existing condition**: Still required — Sun Life checks eligibility even for STD claims

## Common Pitfalls (STD-Specific)

- Using permanent-disability language on an STD claim
- Not providing a return-to-work date or modified duties assessment
- Skipping the pre-existing condition question (Sun Life will follow up, delaying the claim)

Same as `ltd-sunlife.md`.
